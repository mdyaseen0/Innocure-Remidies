About Innocure Remedies

Our Mission

Innocure Remedies is a healthcare startup focused on revolutionizing chronic wound care through innovative, nature-powered solutions. Our flagship product, HerbalPlast, is a polyherbal nanofiber patch, spray, and gel developed to treat diabetic foot ulcers and non-healing wounds more effectively. By combining time-tested Ayurvedic extracts with modern pharmaceutical technologies like nanotechnology and electrospinning, we offer safe, affordable, and eco-friendly alternatives to conventional wound treatments. Our scalable product line addresses a critical gap in diabetic wound care, with prototypes validated through clinical collaborations and recognized at national innovation platforms.
